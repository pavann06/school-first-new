What is School First?

SchoolFirst is an AI and neuroscience-powered platform that helps teachers teach better, students learn better, and stay truly involved. It brings planning, teaching, assessment, communication, and analytics together so schools can focus on what matters most – learning.

SchoolFirst AI

AI that respects the heart of education while sharpening its brain

AI
that makes your school data actually useful

SchoolFirst turns everyday data from attendance, homework, assessments, communication and activities into clear, actionable insights. No complex tools, no manual number crunching – just guidance for better decisions.

AI for Planning

• Suggest academic calendars based on board guidelines and local context.

• Recommend lesson pacing using past performance, events and Time Table data.

• Propose balanced assessment blueprints using patterns from Assessments and Curriculum.

AI for Monitoring

• Spot students at risk by combining Attendance, Assessments, Skills Calendar and behaviour notes.

• Flag classes where curriculum coverage or homework completion is falling behind.

• Highlight grades and subjects that need leadership attention, support or celebration.

AI for Support & Growth

• Suggest targeted remedial tasks, groups and resources through Online Courses, Edutainment and VocabBee.

• Nudge parents with personalised messages and P-Tips to strengthen home support.

• Surface stories and achievements for school branding via Gallery, School Bulletin and School Events.
